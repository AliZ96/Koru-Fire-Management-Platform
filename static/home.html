The Problem
2,800 forest fires. 17 lives lost. ₺48 billion in damage — and the worst part? Most of it was preventable.

İzmir alone saw ~160 forest fires burning over 10,600 hectares. 50,000+ people evacuated in 2025. Emergency teams are still working without pre-defined strategic response plans:

  • ✕
    No data-driven risk zone identification — decisions rely on experience alone
  • ✕
    No pre-calculated optimal routes — teams lose critical minutes en route
  • ✕
    No ground vs. air accessibility analysis — resources deployed blindly
  • ✕
    No simulation tools — every fire is handled reactively, never proactively
The Solution

10 Years of Fire Data. One Predictive Engine.

KORU trains machine learning models on a decade of İzmir's wildfire history — integrating NASA satellite imagery, terrain data, and vegetation indices — then pairs prediction with Simulated Annealing and Genetic Algorithm optimization to generate strategic response routes in under 2 minutes.

From "where might it burn?" to "here's exactly how to respond" — before the fire even starts.

Core Features

5 Core Modules.
One Integrated Platform.

ML Risk Zone Modelling
Trained on 10 years of İzmir wildfire data — satellite imagery, NDVI vegetation indices, topography, and meteorological patterns — to predict where the next fire is most likely to ignite.
Accessibility & Resource Analysis
GIS-powered evaluation of every risk zone — which are reachable by fire trucks, which require helicopter deployment, and where water sources and fire stations are positioned.
SA + Genetic Algorithm Routes
Scenario-based route optimization using Simulated Annealing and Genetic Algorithms. Generates strategic intervention paths in under 2 minutes — factoring in terrain, road access, and resource proximity.
Strategic Planning Dashboard
Interactive map layers, risk heatmaps, scenario simulation, and real-time monitoring. One command center for fire chiefs to visualize threats and coordinate multi-team response.
Cloud-Native Backend API
Built on FastAPI and deployed on Azure. OWASP-secured with role-based access control, designed to serve 50+ concurrent users across municipal fire departments.

How It Works

From Satellite Data to Strategic Routes.

Step 01
10 Years of Fire History Ingested
NASA FIRMS satellite hotspots, İzmir meteorological records, NDVI vegetation indices, and terrain elevation data — all fed into the system.
Step 02
ML Model Predicts Risk Zones
Machine learning algorithms analyze spatial and temporal fire patterns to identify the highest-probability ignition zones across İzmir's 10,600+ hectare fire-prone region.
Step 03
GIS Maps Accessibility
Every predicted risk zone is evaluated: Can fire trucks reach it? Is helicopter deployment needed? Where are the nearest water sources and fire stations?
Step 04
SA + GA Optimizes Response Routes
Simulated Annealing and Genetic Algorithms generate optimal multi-vehicle intervention paths — balancing speed, terrain difficulty, and resource allocation in under 2 minutes.
Step 05
Dashboard Delivers Actionable Plans
Fire chiefs receive ready-to-execute strategic response plans with interactive maps, risk overlays, and scenario comparisons — all before a single tree catches fire.
